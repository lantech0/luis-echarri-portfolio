How It Works

Content That's Built Around
Your Business — Not Recycled
From a Template

Every post I create starts with research. Here's exactly how I turn what I learn about your business into content that gets your customers to stop scrolling.

🔍
01
I Research Your Niche
Before writing anything, I study your industry, your local competitors, and what your customers are already asking online. I find the topics, hooks, and angles that work in your specific market.
🎨
02
I Learn Your Brand
I dig into your tone, your values, and what makes your business different. Every caption and graphic is written to sound like you — not like a generic social media post.
🗓️
03
I Build Your Monthly Calendar
I prepare a full 20-post plan — captions written, graphics designed, posting times set. Every post has a clear purpose: educate, promote, engage, or build trust.
✅
04
You Review and Approve
I send you everything in a shared Google Sheet before anything goes live. You read it, request changes if needed, and approve. Nothing is posted without your sign-off.
⏱️
05
I Schedule Everything
Once you approve, I schedule all posts through Meta Business Suite. You don't lift a finger from here.
📊
06
Monthly Report
At the end of each month I send you a plain-English performance report — what got the most reach, what drove the most engagement, and what we'll do better next month.
